# Privacy Policy
## ibbe & co Private Limited
**Effective Date: 26 Jan 2026**

## 1. Introduction
ibbe & co Private Limited ("we," "our," or "the organisation") operates as a registered private entity in India. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information across our digital platforms.

This policy applies to all websites and services operated by the organisation and its subsidiaries, including ibbe.in, journal.ibbe.in, status.ibbe.in, jobs.ibbe.in, links.ibbe.in, and our URL shortening services, as well as any future platforms we may introduce.

We are committed to transparency in our data practices. This document outlines what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it to deliver and improve our services.

## 2. Scope of This Policy
This Privacy Policy governs all digital platforms operated by ibbe & co Private Limited and its subsidiaries, including:
● ibbe.in – our primary website and public portal
● journal.ibbe.in – our content platform for articles and reading materials
● status.ibbe.in – our system status and service health dashboard
● jobs.ibbe.in – our hiring portal for job applications and recruitment
● links.ibbe.in – our link management and analytics platform
● URL shortening services – our link shortening infrastructure used across our platforms

This policy also applies to any future subdomains, platforms, or services introduced under the ibbe & co brand or its subsidiaries. All platforms maintain consistent privacy practices as described in this document.

We do not collect:
● Health information (except as required by law for employment purposes)
● Financial details (such as bank account or credit card information)
● Precise geolocation data (we collect only region or country-level data)
● Behavioral tracking across third-party websites

● Personal identifying information through our URL shortening services beyond what is described above

## 4. How We Collect Information

### 4.4 Cookies
We deploy cookies to store preferences and support website functionality. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect your experience on our platforms.

We do not use advanced tracking technologies such as fingerprinting, cross-device tracking, or behavioral surveillance tools.

## 7. Data Storage and Security

### 7.1 Storage Infrastructure
We store your information using secure third-party cloud storage and hosting services:

**Website Hosting:**
● ibbe.in is hosted on AWS Amplify in the Mumbai (Asia Pacific) region
● Other websites are hosted on Vercel, a cloud hosting platform
● We plan to migrate certain services to Amazon EC2 (Elastic Compute Cloud) in the Mumbai region for enhanced control and security
● All platforms implement industry-standard security measures including encryption and access controls

**Aadhaar Card Documents:**
● Stored on Amazon S3 (Simple Storage Service) in the Mumbai (Asia Pacific) region in secure, encrypted buckets
● Access is restricted through time-limited signed URLs that expire after 15 minutes
● Access logs are maintained for security monitoring
● Only authorized personnel can access these documents through secure authentication

**Resumes and CVs:**
● Stored on Amazon S3 in the Mumbai (Asia Pacific) region in secure buckets
● Protected through access controls and encryption
● Accessible only to authorized recruitment personnel
● Retained for the duration specified in Section 8

**Profile Photographs:**
● Stored on Bunny.net content delivery network
● Used for employee identification card generation
● Protected through secure access protocols
● Retained for the duration of your profile or employment relationship

**Link Analytics Data:**
● Stored on secure servers with access controls
● Aggregated and analyzed to provide performance insights
● Protected through encryption and authentication mechanisms

### 7.2 Security Measures
We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:
● Encryption for data transmission (SSL/TLS)
● Encryption for data at rest
● Access controls and authentication mechanisms
● Time-limited access to sensitive documents
● Regular security assessments
● Monitoring of system logs
● Restricted access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
● Secure server infrastructure with regular updates and patches

### 7.3 Document Verification
Aadhaar card documents uploaded to jobs.ibbe.in are reviewed and verified by authorized administrators. Upon verification, documents are marked as approved in your profile.

### 7.4 Re-verification Requirements
For jobs.ibbe.in users, we require periodic re-verification of identity documents every 3 months (quarterly). You will receive notifications when re-verification is due, and you may be required to upload fresh copies of identity documents.

## 9. Data Retention
We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

### 9.1 Retention Periods
● Waitlist and newsletter information: Retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion
● Analytics data: Aggregated data retained indefinitely; individual session data retained for 14 months (Google Analytics default)
● Link analytics data: Click data and performance metrics retained for up to 24 months for trend analysis
● Security logs: Retained for up to 12 months
● Email communication logs: Retained in accordance with Resend's data retention policies (typically up to 90 days for delivery logs)
● Job application data (jobs.ibbe.in):
○ Active applications: Retained while your profile is active or until the position is filled
○ Resumes and CVs: Retained for up to 3 years from last application or as required by law
○ Aadhaar card copies: Retained for the duration of active applications plus 3 months, or as required by employment law
○ Profile photographs: Retained while your profile is active and during any period of employment
○ Unsuccessful applications: Retained for up to 1 year unless you request earlier deletion
● Employee records: If hired, employment records are retained in accordance with applicable labor laws and company policy

### 9.2 Deletion
When information is deleted, it is removed from active databases. Residual copies in backup systems are deleted during routine backup cycles (typically within 90 days).
You may request deletion of your profile and associated data from jobs.ibbe.in at any time, subject to legal retention requirements.
