## Naming by Category

Different types of things inside IBBE call for different sources. This table removes the
guesswork.

| Category | Source to Use | Example |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| External programs | The Mark | IBBE Readership, IBBE Forge |
| Internal ranks and titles | The Archive | Senior Reader, Provost, Warden |
| Digital products and platforms | The Standard | Vault, Atlas, Arc, Signal |
| Credentials and certificates | The Archive or The Mark | Licentiate, IBBE Seal, IBBE Vellum |
| Events and gatherings | The Archive or The Mark | Convocation, IBBE Convocation |
| Governance and policy documents | The Archive | Charter, Ordinance, Compendium |
| Internal tools and systems | The Standard | Ledger, Lens, Core |
| Community spaces | The Standard | Common, Anchor |
| Publication series | The Archive or The Mark | Annals, IBBE Folio |
| Membership tiers | The Archive | Matriculant, Liege, Licentiate |
