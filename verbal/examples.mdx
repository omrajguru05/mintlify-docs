### Section 5: Applied Examples (The Humane Standard)

**Purpose:** to show how "stark" and "kind" work together.

#### 5.1 Email: The Welcome Note

**Context:** a user just signed up.

**Goal:** make them feel valued and calm.

**Subject:** you're here. let's begin.

hello [name],

it's wonderful to have you with us.

we built ibbe because we believe education should respect your time and
dignity. we're here to help you bridge the gap between learning a theory and
actually doing the work.

take your time. look around. when you're ready, we're right here.

warm regards,
the ibbe team

#### 5.2 UX Writing: Wrong Password

**Context:** the user failed a login attempt.

**Goal:** reduce frustration. sound helpful, not robotic.

**Headline:** let's try that again.

**Body:** it looks like the password needs a second look. please check your
details and retry.

**Link:** or we can send a reset link to your email.

#### 5.3 Website Header: The Mission

**Context:** the first thing a student sees on the homepage.

**Goal:** simple, confident, inviting.

**Headline:** real skills. real standards.

**Sub-headline:** we replace the confusion of theory with the clarity of practice.
this is education designed for the real world.

**Button:** start exploring

#### 5.4 Formal Letter: To a University Partner

**Context:** asking for a meeting.

**Goal:** confident, elegant, respectful.

dear [name],

we admire the reputation you hold in the education sector.

at ibbe, we share your passion for student competence. we operate a global
standard that helps students translate their academic knowledge into
professional discipline.

we believe a conversation between us could open new doors for your
students. we welcome the opportunity to discuss this further at your
convenience.

with respect,
the ibbe team

#### 5.5 In-App Message: Feature Update

**Context:** introducing a new tool.

**Goal:** exciting but minimal.

**Headline:** meet focus.

**Body:** we created a new way for you to track your progress. it's simple, clean,
and designed to keep you calm while you study.

**Button:** see how it works
