### Section 4: The Vocabulary

**Purpose:** to choose words that feel "fresh" and "valuable."

#### 4.1 The Approved Lexicon

- **Preferred terms:**
  - student (personal and respectful)
  - standard (implies quality)
  - invest (implies future return)
  - guide (implies help)
- **Banned list:**
  - Negatives: no, not, never, can't, won't, don't, shouldn't, nothing, etc.
  - Corporate jargon: synergy, leverage, embark, utilize, facilitate.
  - Urgent sales: hurry, blast, cheap, deal.
