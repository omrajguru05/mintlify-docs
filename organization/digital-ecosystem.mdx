### 2. Data Access Policy

IBBE follows a strict, role-based access model:

- Officers only see what they're working on
- Managers distribute data on a need-to-know basis
- Downloads of internal files are restricted unless flagged as "External Share
Approved"
- No IBBE doc should be circulated outside our ecosystem without watermark and
approval

Breaches are logged, and misuse leads to immediate revocation of role and access keys
